Account & Data Deletion Request

Apna Doctor ("Rathisoft") respects your privacy. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, you can do so directly within the app or by contacting us.

Method 1: Delete In-App (Recommended)

Settings → Delete Account

  1. Open the Apna Doctor app
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon in the bottom navigation)
  3. Scroll to the About section
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Review the list of data that will be permanently deleted
  6. Confirm deletion

Note: This action is irreversible. All data will be permanently removed.

We will process your request within 7 business days.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Profile information (name, email, phone, address, photo)
  • Appointment history and records
  • Medical records (consultations, hospitalisations, prescriptions)
  • Chat messages and media
  • Call history and logs
  • AI chat history
  • Authentication credentials

Data That May Be Retained

  • Anonymous analytics data (if applicable) — not linked to your identity
  • Records required by applicable law or for legal compliance
Important: Deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed. After deletion, you will need to create a new account to use Apna Doctor again.
